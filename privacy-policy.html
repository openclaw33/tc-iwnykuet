Time Control - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Developer: nakamotolabs.timekeeper

Overview

Time Control ("the App") is a timer application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data (your name, email, timer settings, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage.

What We Don't Do

  • We do NOT collect personal information
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking
  • We do NOT show ads
  • We do NOT share data with third parties
  • We do NOT use cookies for tracking

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your display name and email (for the in-app profile)
  • Theme preference (light/dark)
  • Timer statistics (number of timers run, total time)

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect any data from children.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
